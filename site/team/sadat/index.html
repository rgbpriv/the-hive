Sadat Sakif Ahmed leads Arcane Hive as its Lead Strategist and Architect. A system architect and senior software developer with a B.Sc. in Computer Science from BRAC University, he brings over ten years across the full software lifecycle — from concept and system design through to deployment and optimisation — and the leadership to coordinate the cross-functional teams that deliver it.

His track record runs deep. Sadat has built big-data analytics for ringID, one of Bangladesh's first large-scale social platforms; engineered the low-bandwidth video pipeline behind Amader Daktar, the country's first telemedicine app, tuned to work over 2G in rural areas; and developed voice-driven systems that let people complete transactions over an ordinary phone call — no internet or literacy required.

That range is rare. Across full-stack web (Angular, React, Vue, Django), mobile, machine learning, and big-data infrastructure, Sadat can see the whole board — and it shows in Arcane Hive's own proof points: he built the Angular social platform Fanmire.com and is a senior developer on the European learning platform Zabai.no. He chooses the right architecture for your problem, not the one a narrower team happens to know.

It is this breadth and seniority that Sadat brings to every Arcane Hive engagement: the assurance that your solution is built to international standards and engineered for long-term growth — an asset that still serves you years from now, not a liability you will have to replace.
