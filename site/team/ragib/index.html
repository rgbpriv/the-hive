Ragib Ehsan is Arcane Hive's Full Stack & Systems Lead and CTO, specialising in cybersecurity and network security. He studied Network and IT Security at Ontario Tech University in Canada, following earlier studies in Computer & Information Technology at Purdue University, and brings over seven years of hands-on experience across security, networking, and software development.

Ragib's perspective reaches well beyond the code. He has been a founder since his teens — co-founding the Mekdela Computers Foundation to build a computer lab for a local school, and later co-founding Eagle Eye Games, a virtual-reality startup, as its CTO. Having led on both the technical and operational sides of building a company, he reads a project the way an owner does — weighing every technical decision against cost, operations, and return, and speaking the business stack as fluently as the tech stack.

His own craft is the part of a build most owners never see until it fails: keeping systems secure, resilient, and under your control. From network and application security through to full-stack development, Ragib makes sure the platform running your business is protected against threats and downtime — not merely functional on the day it launches.

It is this blend of security-first discipline and a founder's instinct for the business that Ragib brings to every Arcane Hive engagement — your digital infrastructure treated with the same seriousness you give your physical assets: secure, maintained, and genuinely yours.
