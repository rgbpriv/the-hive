Arafat Rahman is Arcane Hive's Production Manager, the person who keeps projects moving from kickoff to delivery. He coordinates the team, tracks the moving parts, and holds the work to a consistent standard, so what's promised to a client actually ships: on time and in shape.

He comes to it with a builder's range. Arafat has led design, coordination, and delivery on a campus utility platform that grew to over 4,000 users with 1,500 daily actives, work that earned runner-up honours at university tech and research showcases, and has coordinated UI/UX and rollout for volunteer-management and event-verification platforms, turning manual workflows into clean, working tools. A designer and digital marketer by craft, he keeps an eye on how the finished product looks and lands, not just whether it runs.

For Arcane Hive's clients, that is the assurance that someone owns the follow-through. Arafat is the point of continuity who keeps your project organised, communicated, and accountable, so it reaches the finish line as promised, not just ‘mostly done.’
