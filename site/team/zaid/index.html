Zaid Fahad

Product & Mobile Solutions Architect

Dhaka, Bangladesh

The builder who turns hardware and software into products people actually use — fluent from low-level embedded systems all the way up to polished mobile and web.

Zaid Fahad is Arcane Hive's Product & Mobile Solutions Architect — a rare full-stack builder whose range runs from low-level embedded systems all the way up to refined mobile and web products. He works fluently across IoT and Linux, cross-platform apps, and interactive web interfaces, with a genuine designer's eye for how all of it feels in a user's hands.

On the systems side, Zaid has authored low-level Linux services in C to keep IoT devices and databases in sync, wired device telemetry over MQTT for real-time diagnostics, and built self-service kiosk systems — from autonomous retail checkout to healthcare appointment terminals — including biometric identity verification with on-device facial recognition and document OCR. He has also developed embedded interfaces for automotive-grade Linux.

He leads products end to end, too. As product lead for a campus utility app, Zaid scaled it past 4,000 users with 1,500 daily actives — shipping course planning, faculty directories, and more; and as IoT lead on a drone-telemetry platform, he built low-latency, fail-safe real-time monitoring for UAV operations. Paired with strong UI/UX and brand-design skills, it means the tools he builds get adopted, not abandoned.

For Arcane Hive's clients, that translates into solutions that work in the real world — on the factory floor, at the counter, and in your customers' hands. Whether it is IoT monitoring, a mobile app, or a self-service system, Zaid makes sure it is intuitive, reliable, and genuinely usable from day one.
