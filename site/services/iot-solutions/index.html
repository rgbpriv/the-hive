Frequently asked

Questions, answered.

How does IoT monitoring prevent theft and loss in warehouses?

Our monitoring infrastructure tracks the state of your physical assets continuously and flags problems the moment something moves, fails, or goes missing, so you act before it costs you. Instead of discovering shrinkage at the next stock count, management gets real-time alerts and a record of exactly what happened and when.

What kind of IoT devices does Arcane Hive build?

We research and build hardware locally, from smart-monitoring sensors for factory floors to self-service kiosks and AI-integrated machines such as our juice cart and sun-emulating spice dryer. Because devices are engineered here, they fit your environment, budget, and floor rather than a template imported from abroad.

Can IoT really lower our running costs?

Yes, wherever there is waste to find. Energy and usage monitoring turns raw data into decisions, surfacing the loads quietly eating your margins so you can bring them under control. The system pays for itself once the savings and prevented losses outweigh the deployment cost.
